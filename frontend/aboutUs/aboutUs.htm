How We Started



FasTech is a newly established business. It was formed by Filipino businessman Joshua Contreras and co-founders Natalia Andaya, Erickson John Elopre, Keem Catolico, and Aaron Christian Felias. FasTech serves a variety of high quality computer hardware and software at affordable price. FasTech marketing support provides local store marketing opportunities and introduces new products that capture the hearts of our custo mers.












We create our Fastech website in order to sell our product through it customers are able to browse the catalog and select the products they desire. The selected items can be collected in a shopping cart when the customers click on a product, they will be redirected to the products’ details page where the customers can then select the quantity they want, and add the product to their cart. At checkout time, the items in the shopping cart will be presented as an order. At that time, more information will be needed to complete the transaction. Usually, the customer will be asked to fill or select a billing address, a shipping address, a shipping option, and payment information such as a credit card number. Once the needed information has been filled, an e-mail confirmation is sent to the customer as soon as the order is placed and ready to be processed.
